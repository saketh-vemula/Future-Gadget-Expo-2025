    Event Location

    Moscone Convention Center

    Address: 747 Howard Street, San Francisco, CA 94103

    The Moscone Convention Center is San Francisco’s premier event venue, located in the heart of the city’s SOMA district. With state-of-the-art facilities and easy access to public transportation, hotels, and restaurants, it’s the perfect venue for our technology expo.

    • BART*: Montgomery St. or Powell St. stations (10-minute walk)
    • Muni*: Multiple bus lines serve the area
    • Parking*: Several parking garages nearby
    • Airport*: 30-minute drive from SFO
